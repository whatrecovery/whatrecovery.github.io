A national campaign for full employment, good jobs, and a reformed Federal Reserve.

We stand with millions of workers and their families in calling on the Federal Reserve to adopt pro-worker policies for the rest of us. The Fed can keep interest rates low; give the economy a fair chance to recover; and prioritize full employment and rising wages.

The economy is still not working for us.

The economy
is still not
working for us.

The truth about the economy is obvious to most of us: not enough jobs, not enough hours, and not enough pay. This is particularly true in communities of color and among young workers.

Some members of the Federal Reserve refuse to see that truth. They think that the economy has recovered. They want to raise interest rates to slow down job increases and prevent wages from rising faster.

That’s a terrible idea. The Fed needs to publicly commit to keeping interest rates low until all communities experience a full recovery, and everybody who wants to can find a good job.

Our voices need to be heard

Our voices
need to be heard

It’s our economy too, and when big decisions on the economy are made, our voices need to be heard. After all, the Fed is supposed to represent the public interest.

The Federal Reserve System is dominated by big banks and corporations, and regular folks who are struggling in the economy don’t have a say.

If we want to build an economy that works for all of us, that needs to change. The decisions of the Fed are too important to leave to major corporations and Wall Street.
